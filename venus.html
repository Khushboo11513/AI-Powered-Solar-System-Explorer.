♀️ Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is similar in size and structure to Earth. Its diameter is about 12,104 kilometers (7,521 miles), making it the sixth-largest planet in the solar system.

Key facts about Venus:

  • ☀️ Second from the Sun: Venus is the second planet from the Sun, orbiting it in 225 Earth days.
  • 🌎 Similar to Earth: Venus is often called Earth's "sister planet" because of its similar size and composition.
  • 🌪️ Thick atmosphere: Venus has a thick atmosphere, mostly made of carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid.
  • 🔥 Extreme temperatures: Venus has surface temperatures around 460°C (860°F), hotter than Mercury despite being farther from the Sun.
  • ⏳ Slow rotation: A day on Venus lasts 243 Earth days, but its year (orbital period) is only 225 Earth days.
  • 🌀 Venusian winds: Venus experiences winds that can reach speeds of 360 kilometers per hour (224 miles per hour) in its upper atmosphere.
