♂️ Mars

Mars, often called the "Red Planet," is the fourth planet from the Sun. It has a diameter of about 6,779 kilometers (4,212 miles), roughly half the size of Earth. Its reddish appearance is due to iron oxide (rust) on its surface.

Key Facts about Mars:

  • 🔴 Red Planet: Mars is known for its reddish hue, caused by iron oxide (rust) on its surface.
  • 🌍 Half the size of Earth: Mars has a diameter of 6,779 kilometers, about half of Earth's diameter.
  • 🌒 Two Moons: Mars has two small moons, Phobos and Deimos, which are irregularly shaped.
  • 🌡️ Cold and dry: Mars has temperatures as low as -125°C (-195°F) and a thin atmosphere, mostly made of carbon dioxide.
  • ⏳ Day similar to Earth: A day on Mars lasts 24.6 hours, almost identical to an Earth day.
  • 🏜️ Largest volcano: Mars is home to Olympus Mons, the largest volcano in the solar system, standing 22 kilometers (13.6 miles) high.
