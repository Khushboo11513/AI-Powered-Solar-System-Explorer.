☿️ Mercury

Mercury is a small, rocky planet with a diameter of about 4,880 kilometers (about 3,032 miles), making it the smallest planet in the solar system, only slightly larger than the Moon.

Key facts about Earth:

  • ☀️ Closest to the Sun: Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun.
  • 🌑 Smallest planet: Mercury is the smallest planet in the solar system.
  • 🌙 No moons: Mercury does not have any moons or rings.
  • 🌡️ Extreme temperatures: Daytime temperatures can reach 430°C, and nighttime temperatures can drop to -180°C.
  • ⚡ Weak magnetic field: Mercury has a weak magnetic field, about 1% the strength of Earth’s.
  • ⏳ Slow rotation: A day on Mercury lasts about 176 Earth days, longer than its year (88 Earth days).
