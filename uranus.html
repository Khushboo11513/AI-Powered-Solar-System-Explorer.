🪐 Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and is unique for its blue-green color due to the methane in its atmosphere. It is an ice giant with extreme weather patterns and a tilted rotation.

Key facts about Uranus:

  • 🌌 Ice Giant: Uranus is composed mainly of water, ammonia, and methane ice.
  • 🌍 Unique Rotation: Uranus rotates on its side, with an axial tilt of 98°.
  • 🌑 14 Moons: Uranus has 14 known moons, with Miranda, Ariel, and Titania being the largest.
  • 🌡️ Cold Atmosphere: The atmosphere is composed of hydrogen, helium, and methane, and is very cold, with temperatures around -224°C.
  • ⚡ Weak Magnetic Field: Uranus has a magnetic field that is tilted and offset from its center.
  • 🌀 Extreme Weather: Uranus experiences violent storms and high winds, reaching up to 900 km/h (560 mph).
