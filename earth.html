🌍 Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life. It has a diverse environment with water, air, and land. Earth's atmosphere is primarily composed of nitrogen and oxygen, making it habitable for living organisms.

Key facts about Earth:

  • 🌊 71% water: Mostly in the form of oceans.
  • 🌕 One Moon: Earth's natural satellite.
  • 🌦️ Dynamic climate: Weather patterns and seasons.
  • 🧲 Magnetic field: Protects from harmful solar radiation.
