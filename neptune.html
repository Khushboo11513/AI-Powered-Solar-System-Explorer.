🌊 Neptune

Neptune, the eighth planet from the Sun, is known for its deep blue color and strong winds. It is a gas giant with an intense atmosphere and mysterious weather patterns.

Key Facts About Neptune:

  • 🌌 Farthest Planet: Neptune is 30.1 AU away from the Sun.
  • 💨 Strong Winds: Winds on Neptune reach speeds of up to 2,100 km/h.
  • 🌑 Great Dark Spot: A giant storm similar to Jupiter's Great Red Spot.
  • 🌍 Moons: 16 moons, with Triton being the largest.
  • 🌡️ Cold Atmosphere: Neptune's surface temperature is around -214°C.
  • ⚡ Magnetic Field: Strong and tilted at 47°.
